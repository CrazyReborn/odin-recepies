Bacon-Tomato-Lettace (BLT) Sandwich

Go Back To Main

Discription

Classic ammerican sandwich made of 3 main ingredients: bacon, tomato and lettuce.

ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Grill bacon on a pan.
  2. While grilling bacon take two pieces of bread and put mayonnasise on one side of each bread. Put them in a toaster.
  3. Take a tomato and cut it.
  4. Take bread from toaster.
  5. Put a piece of lettuce on the first bread. Take bacon off the grill/pan and put it on the lettuce.
  6. Put tomato on bacon, salt it and cover with the second piece of bread.
